Cheese Toast

The best breakfast

The easiest and most delicious breakfast is my famous cheese toast. Do you like cheese, do you like toast? if so you will love this cheese toast.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Place the cheese on the bread
  3. Put the cheese and toast in the oven
  4. Bake for 6 minutes or untill cheese is melted
  5. ENJOY!
