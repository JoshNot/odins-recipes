Vegitable Soup

Vegitable soup is a very easy dish to make and it will warm you right up on a cold winter day. This dish is right even if its not cold out. Maybe its just soup kinda day. Either way you will enjoy this one

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Corn
  • Celery
  • Beans(your choice)
  • Basil
  • Oregeno
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add 8 cups of water to a medium size pot
  2. Chop up all of your vegitables
  3. Add the vegitables to the water
  4. Chop the Basil, Oregeno, and Garlic cloves
  5. Add the herbs to the mix
  6. Bring the mix to a boil
  7. Boil until vegitables are soft
  8. ENJOY!
