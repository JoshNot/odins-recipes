Pasta Recipe

The best pasta recipe you will find on the internet

If you like pasta then this dish is right for you. Its has proven proven to be the best time and time again. It is very easy to make and fun as well. Let me show you the way I make it

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • homemade or store bought pasta
  • tomato paste
  • whole tomato
  • garlic cloves
  • basil leaves
  • oregeno leaves
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. add salt, pepper, and basil leaves to the beef
  3. Roll meat into balls and place into the fridge
  4. Add tomato paste to a shallow pan
  5. Cut galic cloves into small pieces and add to the paste
  6. Sprinkle chopped basil and oregeno into the paste
  7. Chop the tomato up and add to the paste
  8. Let the sause simmer
  9. Start boiling the pasta noodles
  10. Place the meatballs in the oven for 22 minutes
  11. Mix the sause and pasta
  12. Place the meat on top
  13. ENJOY!!
